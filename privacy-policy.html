Privacy Policy — Centering Rentals

App: Centering Rentals (JK Centering), package com.devfoundryworks.centering · Developer: DevFoundryWorks
Last updated: 10 July 2026

This policy describes how the Centering Rentals app collects, uses, and protects information. It is based on what the app actually does, verified against its source code.

1. Information We Collect

Centering Rentals is a business-management app for equipment-rental companies. To provide its functionality, the app collects and stores:

  • Account information: the email address or phone number you use to sign in (via Supabase Authentication), and, if you choose Google Sign-In, the basic profile information Google provides for that sign-in method.
  • Business data you enter, including: customer (“party”) records (name, phone number(s), address, and optionally a profile photo you select); company and branch details (company name, address, GSTIN tax identifier, company code); vendor and site records (name, phone, address); inventory records (item names, quantities, specifications); rental, payment, quotation, and agreement records (amounts, dates, payment methods, business terms); and staff/user role assignments within your company (for role-based access control).
  • Device contacts (optional, user-initiated only): if you use the “import from contacts” option when adding a customer, the app reads the single contact you select to prefill that customer's name and phone number. The app does not read, upload, or scan your full contact list — only the specific contact you choose, when you choose it.
  • Diagnostic information you choose to share: the app keeps a local crash/error log on your device. If you use the “Share Diagnostic Logs” feature, that log is shared via your device's own share sheet at your explicit action — it is not automatically transmitted anywhere.

What we do not collect: the app does not access your device's camera, does not access your precise or approximate location, and does not request broad file/storage access (photo selection uses your device's standard file picker, which grants access only to the file you pick).

2. Authentication

Sign-in is handled by Supabase Authentication (email/phone + password, or Google Sign-In via Android's Credential Manager). Your session is stored encrypted on your device (Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences, backed by the Android Keystore) and is never stored in plain text.

3. Data Storage

  • Your business data is stored on our backend, hosted on Supabase (a third-party infrastructure provider that acts as our service provider, not a separate recipient of your data for its own purposes).
  • The app also keeps a local cache of your company's data on your device (Android's Room database) so it continues to work offline, syncing back to our backend once you reconnect.
  • All network traffic between the app and our backend is encrypted (HTTPS/TLS); the app is configured to refuse any unencrypted connection.
  • Android's automatic device backup is disabled for this app, so app data is not included in phone-level cloud backups.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertising networks — this app does not display ads and does not integrate any advertising SDK. Your business data is only accessible to:

  • Your own company's authorized staff, according to the roles and permissions your company administrator assigns within the app.
  • DevFoundryWorks, as the operator of the backend infrastructure the app depends on, solely to provide and maintain the service.

Analytics and crash reporting: the app includes Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics libraries, but in this release they are inactive — no analytics events or crash reports are transmitted to any third party, because the Firebase project configuration required to activate them has not been set up. If this changes in a future release, this policy and the Play Console Data Safety disclosures will be updated first, before any such collection begins.

5. Security

  • All data in transit is encrypted (TLS/HTTPS only).
  • Your authentication session is stored encrypted at rest on your device.
  • The app's login screen blocks screenshots and screen recording on release builds, to reduce credential-exposure risk.
  • Access to your company's data is restricted to authenticated users with an appropriate role, enforced on our backend (not just in the app).

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take reasonable, verifiable steps consistent with the above.

6. Your Responsibilities

  • Keep your login credentials confidential; you are responsible for activity on your account.
  • Only enter contact details for individuals you have a legitimate business relationship with (e.g., your actual customers and vendors) — this app is a business tool, not a general-purpose contact or marketing database.
  • If you are a company administrator, you are responsible for assigning appropriate roles/permissions to your staff and for ensuring your staff's use of the app complies with applicable data-protection laws for your own customers' information.

7. Account & Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your Centering Rentals account and associated personal data at any time:

  • In the app: Settings → Account → Request account deletion.
  • By email: email devfoundryworks@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account.
  • Web page: see our Account Deletion page for full instructions (usable without the app installed).

Deleting your personal account removes your login identity and profile and revokes your access to company workspaces. Business records owned by a company (customers, rentals, payments, invoices, etc.) remain that company's data; deleting your personal account does not delete a company's business data. Company owners can request removal of an entire company workspace via a separate, controlled in-app workflow. Requests are typically processed within 30 days of verification.
