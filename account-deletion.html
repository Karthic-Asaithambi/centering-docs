Delete your Centering Rentals account

App: Centering Rentals (JK Centering) · Developer: DevFoundryWorks

You can request deletion of your Centering Rentals account and its associated personal data at any time, either from inside the app or using this page (for example, if you no longer have the app installed).

Option 1 — In the app

Open the app, go to Settings → Account → Request account deletion, and confirm. Your request is submitted for review and you are signed out.

Option 2 — By email (no app needed)

Email devfoundryworks@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account, with the subject “Account deletion request”. State that you want your account and associated personal data deleted. We verify that the request comes from the account owner and then process it.

What is deleted

  • Your login / account identity and the personal profile data tied to it.
  • Your access to any company workspaces you belonged to.

What is retained

  • Business records owned by a company (customers, rentals, payments, invoices, etc.) are the company's data and are retained under that company's ownership; deleting your personal account does not delete a company's business data. If you are a company owner and want the whole company workspace removed, request company deletion from within the app (Company settings), which follows a separate, controlled workflow.
  • Records we are required to keep for legal, tax, or accounting reasons may be retained for the period required by law.

How long it takes

Requests are typically processed within 30 days of verification.
